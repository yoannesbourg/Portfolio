Me

This is Yoann, passionate FrontEnd Developper. Half French half Brazilian and living in Spain.


I started my web career as a freelance Webdesigner the beginning of 2019, creating mock-ups and designs for French clients’ websites. Consequently, I learnt how to easily create good looking web pages with CMS’s like Wordpress or Prestashop. However, I started being curious about web development when my clients requested very precise changes making me customise these websites with Html, Css and Javascript.


I wanted to progress in my career and upgrade my developpement skills, so I started a ‘self bootcamp following the Codecademy ‘Full Stack Engenieer’ path. The course would take 6 months, but I’m doing it in 3 months, coding 10 hours per day in a coworking full of devs, while I’m doing personal projects and being coached by two experienced developpers.
